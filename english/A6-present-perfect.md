# A6 – Present Perfect (Experience / Result / Duration)

## Purpose

Present Perfect giúp bạn nói về **quá khứ có liên quan tới hiện tại**: (1) trải nghiệm, (2) kết quả hiện tại, (3) kéo dài tới bây giờ.

---

## Key points

- **Form:** have/has + V3 (past participle)
- 3 use-cases chính:
  1) **Experience** (đã từng/chưa từng)
  2) **Result** (kết quả tới hiện tại)
  3) **Duration** (từ… tới giờ)
- **Time markers** hay đi kèm:
  - Experience: **ever, never, before, (not) yet**
  - Result: **just, already, (not) yet**
  - Duration: **since, for, so far, up to now, until now**
- **BẪY LỚN #1:** không dùng Present Perfect với **mốc thời gian quá khứ đã kết thúc**: *yesterday, last week, in 2020…*
- **BẪY LỚN #2:** dùng sai **since vs for**
  - since + **mốc** (since 2020 / since Monday)
  - for + **khoảng thời gian** (for 3 years)
- **BẪY LỚN #3:** quên V3 / dùng V2 thay vì V3
- **Have been to vs have gone to**:
  - **have been to** = đã đi và đã quay về
  - **have gone to** = đã đi và chưa về (hiện đang ở đó)

---

## Rule summary

| Type | Form | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Positive** | have/has + V3 | đã… (liên quan hiện tại) | I have finished my work. |
| **Negative** | haven’t/hasn’t + V3 | chưa… | She hasn’t called me yet. |
| **Question** | Have/Has + S + V3? | đã… chưa? | Have you ever tried sushi? |

**Use-case quick map:**
- **Experience:** Have you ever…? / I’ve never…
- **Result:** I’ve just… / already… / not yet…
- **Duration:** I’ve lived here **since**… / **for**…

---

## Examples

### 1) Experience (đã từng/chưa từng)
1. I **have been** to Da Nang twice.
2. She **has never tried** Korean food.
3. **Have** you **ever seen** snow?

### 2) Result (kết quả ở hiện tại)
4. I **have just finished** my homework.
5. They **have already sent** the email.
6. He **hasn’t replied** yet.

### 3) Duration (kéo dài tới bây giờ)
7. We **have lived** here **for** five years.
8. I **have studied** English **since** 2023.

### 4) Have been to vs have gone to
9. My sister **has gone to** the supermarket. (she’s there now)
10. My sister **has been to** the supermarket. (she came back)

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| I have seen him yesterday. | I saw him yesterday. | Có mốc quá khứ đã kết thúc → Past Simple. |
| She has went to Japan. | She has gone to Japan. | V3 của “go” là **gone**. |
| I have lived here since 3 years. | I have lived here for 3 years. | **since + mốc**, **for + khoảng**. |
| Have you ever went there? | Have you ever been there? / …ever gone there? | Sau have/has dùng **V3**; “been there” tự nhiên nhất. |
| I already finished it. | I have already finished it. | Thiếu have/has + V3. |
| He didn’t call me yet. | He hasn’t called me yet. | “yet” (chưa) thường đi với Present Perfect. |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền have/has + V3 (Experience) (5 câu)

1. I __________ (see) that movie three times.
2. She __________ never __________ (try) sushi.
3. __________ you ever __________ (be) to Thailand?
4. We __________ (meet) him before.
5. They __________ (not/read) this book.

### Part 2: just / already / yet (Result) (5 câu)

6. I have __________ (just/finish) my report.
7. He has __________ (already/send) the files.
8. We haven’t decided __________.
9. Has she called you __________?
10. They have __________ (not/arrive) __________.

### Part 3: since / for (Duration) (5 câu)

11. I have studied English __________ 2023.
12. She has worked here __________ two months.
13. We have known each other __________ a long time.
14. He has lived in this city __________ he was a child.
15. They have been married __________ 10 years.

---

## Answer key

1. **have seen**
2. **has / tried**
3. **Have / been**
4. **have met**
5. **haven’t read**

6. **just finished**
7. **already sent**
8. **yet**
9. **yet**
10. **haven’t arrived yet**

11. **since**
12. **for**
13. **for**
14. **since**
15. **for**

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) **Experience:** Viết 5 câu về trải nghiệm (2 ever-questions + 3 statements).
2) **Result:** Viết 5 câu về kết quả hiện tại (dùng just/already/yet).
3) **Duration:** Viết 5 câu về việc kéo dài tới bây giờ (since/for).
4) Viết 5 câu trộn cả 3 use-cases (mỗi use-case ít nhất 1 câu).
5) Viết 4 câu dùng **have been to / have gone to** (2 câu mỗi loại).
