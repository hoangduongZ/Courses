# A8 – Future (will vs going to)

## Purpose

A8 giúp bạn nói về tương lai **đúng sắc thái**: khi nào là **quyết định ngay lúc nói / dự đoán chung** (will) và khi nào là **kế hoạch đã định / dự đoán có dấu hiệu** (be going to).

---

## Key points

- **will + V1**
  - quyết định **ngay lúc nói**
  - lời hứa / đề nghị / yêu cầu
  - dự đoán **chung** (không dựa vào dấu hiệu rõ)
- **be going to + V1**
  - **kế hoạch/ý định** đã có trước
  - dự đoán dựa trên **evidence (dấu hiệu)**
- **BẪY #1:** nhầm “plan” thành will (nếu đã định trước → going to)
- **BẪY #2:** dùng going to cho quyết định bột phát (thường → will)
- **BẪY #3:** quên “be” trong going to: *I going to…* (sai)
- **Time markers** (không quyết định 100% nhưng hay gặp): tomorrow, next week, soon, later

---

## Rule summary

| Meaning | Form | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Decision now (bột phát) | will + V1 | I’m thirsty. I **will buy** some water. |
| Promise / offer | will + V1 | I **will help** you. |
| Prediction (general) | will + V1 | I think it **will rain** someday this week. |
| Plan/intention (đã định) | am/is/are going to + V1 | I **am going to study** tonight. |
| Prediction (evidence) | am/is/are going to + V1 | Look at those clouds! It **is going to rain**. |

**Negative / Question**
- will not = **won’t**
- be going to: **am not/isn’t/aren’t going to**
- Questions: **Will + S + V1?** / **Am/Is/Are + S + going to + V1?**

---

## Examples

### 1) Decision now (will)
1. I forgot my phone. I **will go back** and get it.
2. The bag looks heavy. I **will carry** it for you.

### 2) Promise / offer (will)
3. Don’t worry. I **won’t tell** anyone.
4. I **will call** you tonight, I promise.

### 3) Plan/intention (going to)
5. I **am going to visit** my grandparents this weekend.
6. She **is going to start** a new course next month.

### 4) Evidence prediction (going to)
7. The sky is very dark. It **is going to rain**.
8. Be careful! You **are going to fall**.

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| I going to study tonight. | I **am** going to study tonight. | going to phải có **be**. |
| I will visit my parents this weekend. (plan đã có) | I’m going to visit my parents this weekend. | Kế hoạch đã định → going to. |
| Look! It will rain! (có dấu hiệu rõ) | Look! It’s going to rain! | Dự đoán có evidence → going to. |
| Will you going to come? | Are you going to come? | Câu hỏi going to dùng **Am/Is/Are**. |
| I won’t to go. | I won’t go. | won’t + V1 (không “to”). |
| She is going to can drive. | She is going to drive. | going to + V1, không dùng modal sau going to. |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn will hoặc going to (10 câu)

1. I think it __________ (rain) tomorrow. (no strong evidence)
2. Look at those clouds! It __________ (rain).
3. I’m tired. I __________ (go) to bed now.
4. We __________ (travel) to Da Lat next week. (plan booked)
5. This box is too heavy. I __________ (help) you.
6. He __________ (not/forget) your birthday. (promise)
7. Be careful! You __________ (drop) your phone!
8. A: The phone is ringing. B: I __________ (answer) it.
9. She __________ (study) abroad after graduation. (intention)
10. I’m sure you __________ (pass) the test. (general prediction)

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

11. I going to buy a new laptop next month. → __________
12. Look! The glass will fall! → __________
13. Will you going to join us? → __________
14. I won’t to be late. → __________
15. We will meet at 7 PM tomorrow. (plan fixed) → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **will rain**
2. **is going to rain**
3. **will go**
4. **are going to travel**
5. **will help**
6. **won’t forget**
7. **are going to drop**
8. **will answer**
9. **is going to study**
10. **will pass**

11. **I am going to buy a new laptop next month.**
12. **Look! The glass is going to fall!**
13. **Are you going to join us?**
14. **I won’t be late.**
15. **We are going to meet at 7 PM tomorrow.**

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết 5 câu “decision now” dùng **will** (tình huống bột phát).
2) Viết 5 câu “plan” dùng **going to** (kế hoạch đã có).
3) Viết 5 câu dự đoán: 2 câu **will** (general) + 3 câu **going to** (evidence).
4) Viết 5 câu hỏi: 2 câu **Will you…?** + 3 câu **Are you going to…?**
5) Viết đoạn 6 câu kế hoạch cuối tuần: dùng cả will và going to.
