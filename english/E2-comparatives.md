# E2 – Comparatives (adj/adv)
> Level: newbie • Focus: so sánh đúng và tự nhiên (comparative / superlative / as…as)

---

## Purpose
Giúp bạn so sánh người/vật/hành động rõ ràng trong nói & viết: “hơn”, “nhất”, “bằng”, tránh sai dạng tính từ/trạng từ và dùng đúng cấu trúc.

## Key points
- So sánh 2 đối tượng: **comparative** (`-er / more`) + `than`.
- So sánh “nhất” trong nhóm: **superlative** (`the -est / the most`).
- `as + adj/adv + as` = “bằng”; phủ định thường dùng `not as/so … as`.
- 1 âm tiết thường thêm `-er/-est` (fast → faster → fastest). 2+ âm tiết thường dùng `more/most` (important → more important → most important).
- Quy tắc chính tả: big → bigger; happy → happier; nice → nicer.
- Bẫy: phân biệt **adj** vs **adv** (good vs well; fast có thể là cả adj/adv).
- Irregular: **good/well → better → best**, **bad → worse → worst**, **far → farther/further → farthest/furthest**.
- Trong so sánh, đại từ sau `than` trong giao tiếp thường dùng object form: *than me/him/us*.

## Rule summary
### 1) Comparative (so sánh hơn)
- `adj/adv + -er + than` (short)
- `more + adj/adv + than` (long)

### 2) Superlative (so sánh nhất)
- `the + adj + -est` (short)
- `the most + adj` (long)

### 3) Equality (bằng)
- `as + adj/adv + as`
- `not as/so + adj/adv + as`

## Examples
### A) Comparatives (2 things)
1. This laptop is **lighter than** my old one.
2. She is **more patient than** her brother.
3. He works **faster than** me.

### B) Superlatives (in a group)
4. This is **the cheapest** option.
5. She is **the most experienced** person on the team.

### C) as…as / not as…as
6. My phone is **as good as** yours.
7. This task is **not as difficult as** I expected.

### D) Irregular
8. Today is **better** than yesterday.
9. That’s the **worst** idea.

## Common mistakes
1. Sai: *more faster* → Đúng: **faster** (không dùng cả more + -er)
2. Sai: *the most cheapest* → Đúng: **the cheapest**
3. Sai: *She is more tall than me.* → Đúng: **taller** (tall → -er)
4. Sai: *He is gooder than me.* → Đúng: **better**
5. Sai: *as more expensive as* → Đúng: **as expensive as**
6. Sai: *She drives more careful than him.* → Đúng: **more carefully** (adv) / **more careful** (adj + noun)

## Practice A
> 15 câu (điền/chọn/sửa lỗi). Chọn đáp án đúng nhất.

1) My new job is ___ (more busy / busier) than my old job.

2) This app is ___ (more useful / usefuller) than that one.

3) Today is ___ (hotter / more hot) than yesterday.

4) She is the ___ (best / better) student in the class.

5) This is the ___ (most important / more important) part.

6) My salary isn’t as ___ (high / higher) as his.

7) He runs ___ (faster / more faster) than me.

8) A motorbike is generally ___ (cheaper / the cheapest) than a car.

9) This solution is ___ (more simple / simpler) than the old one.

10) That was the ___ (worse / worst) day of my life.

11) I feel ___ (better / best) now, thank you.

12) She speaks English ___ (more fluently / more fluent) than I do.

13) (Fix the sentence) *This is the most easiest question.*

14) (Fix the sentence) *My room is more small than yours.*

15) Choose the correct meaning of the sentence:
“Java is not as easy as Python.”
A. Java is easier than Python.
B. Python is easier than Java.

## Answer key
1) **busier** — busy → busier
2) **more useful** — 2+ syllables
3) **hotter** — hot → hotter
4) **best** — superlative
5) **most important** — superlative long adjective
6) **high** — as…as uses base form
7) **faster** — no “more faster”
8) **cheaper** — general comparison
9) **simpler** — simple → simpler (common form)
10) **worst** — superlative of bad
11) **better** — comparative/irregular (state now vs before)
12) **more fluently** — adv comparison
13) **This is the easiest question.** — remove “most”
14) **My room is smaller than yours.** — small → smaller
15) **B** — “not as…as” means the second is easier

## Practice B
> 5 bài viết (free writing) theo template.

1) Viết 6 câu so sánh 2 thứ trong công việc/học tập (dùng `-er` hoặc `more`).
2) Viết 4 câu dùng `as…as` (2 câu khẳng định, 2 câu phủ định `not as/so…as`).
3) Viết 5 câu superlative về “best/worst/most…” trong team/công ty/lớp.
4) Viết 4 câu dùng irregular forms: better/best, worse/worst, farther/further.
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6–8 câu so sánh 2 công nghệ (vd: Java vs Python / Angular vs React). Bắt buộc có: 2 comparative + 1 superlative + 2 as…as.

## Self-check rubric
- Tôi không dùng double comparative/superlative (more faster, most easiest).
- Tôi chọn đúng `-er/-est` vs `more/most` theo độ dài tính từ.
- Tôi dùng đúng `as…as` với base form.
- Tôi phân biệt adj vs adv khi so sánh hành động.
- Tôi dùng đúng irregular forms (better/best, worse/worst, farther/further).
