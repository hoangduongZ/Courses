# C0 – Subject–Verb Agreement (S-V Agreement)

## Purpose

C0 giúp bạn tránh lỗi nền tảng nhất khi viết tiếng Anh: **chủ ngữ số ít/số nhiều quyết định động từ**. Fix được C0 thì các task về thì (tenses) và viết đoạn sẽ sạch lỗi hơn rất nhiều.

---

## Key points

- **He/She/It + V-s/es** (present simple): *He works.*
- **I/You/We/They + V1**: *They work.*
- **Plural noun → V1**: *Students study.*
- **Uncountable noun → singular verb**: *Information is…*
- **There is / There are**: verb theo danh từ phía sau.
- **Either/Neither + singular** (thường): *Either option is fine.*
- **A number of + plural** vs **The number of + singular**.
- **BẪY:** danh từ số ít nhưng “trông giống số nhiều” (news, math, physics) → **singular**.

---

## Rule summary

### 1) Present Simple core
- **Số ít (he/she/it, singular countable noun)**: V-s/es
- **Số nhiều (they/we, plural noun)**: V1

### 2) Common patterns
- **Uncountable N + singular verb**: *Water is…*
- **There is/are + N**: *There are many reasons.*
- **A number of + plural N + plural verb**: *A number of students are late.*
- **The number of + plural N + singular verb**: *The number of students is increasing.*

---

## Examples

### 1) Basic present simple
1. She **works** in IT.
2. They **work** in IT.

### 2) Uncountable nouns
3. Advice **is** useful.
4. Information **is** important.

### 3) There is / There are
5. There **is** a problem.
6. There **are** two problems.

### 4) Either/Neither
7. Either answer **is** acceptable.
8. Neither option **works** for me.

### 5) A number of vs The number of
9. A number of people **are** waiting.
10. The number of people **is** increasing.

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| He go to work. | He **goes** to work. | he/she/it + V-s/es |
| People is friendly. | People **are** friendly. | “people” = plural |
| The news are bad. | The news **is** bad. | “news” singular |
| There is many reasons. | There **are** many reasons. | theo danh từ sau |
| A number of students is late. | A number of students **are** late. | “a number of” = plural |
| The number of students are rising. | The number of students **is** rising. | “the number of” = singular |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

**Part 1: Chọn đúng động từ (10 câu)**

1. He __________ (work/works) every day.
2. They __________ (need/needs) more time.
3. My parents __________ (live/lives) in the countryside.
4. The teacher __________ (teach/teaches) English.
5. Students __________ (study/studies) hard before exams.

6. Information __________ (is/are) easy to share.
7. The news __________ (is/are) surprising.
8. There __________ (is/are) a lot of traffic today.
9. There __________ (is/are) many ways to solve this.
10. Either option __________ (work/works) for me.

**Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)**

11. She don’t like coffee. → __________
12. The team are winning. → __________
13. A number of books is missing. → __________
14. The number of employees are growing. → __________
15. Each students have a laptop. → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **works**
2. **need**
3. **live**
4. **teaches**
5. **study**

6. **is**
7. **is**
8. **is**
9. **are**
10. **works**

11. **She doesn’t like coffee.**
12. **The team is winning.** *(team như 1 tập thể → singular, kiểu Mỹ; kiểu Anh có thể plural nhưng mình chọn 1 chuẩn để học)*
13. **A number of books are missing.**
14. **The number of employees is growing.**
15. **Each student has a laptop.**

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết 10 câu Present Simple: 5 câu **he/she/it + V-s** + 5 câu **they/we + V1**.
2) Viết 10 câu với **uncountable nouns** (information/advice/money/furniture…)
3) Viết 6 câu dùng **there is/there are** (3 singular + 3 plural).
4) Viết 6 câu dùng **each/every/either/neither** (chú ý singular verb).
5) Viết 4 câu phân biệt **a number of** vs **the number of**.
