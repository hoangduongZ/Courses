# Practice A3 - Past Simple

## Practice A

### Part 1: Điền V2/V-ed (5 câu)

1. I went (go) to the cinema last night.
2. She studied (study) English for 3 hours yesterday.
3. They didn't like (not/like) the food at the restaurant.
4. We saw (see) a beautiful sunset last evening.
5. He worked (work) late on Friday.

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

6. I didn't saw him yesterday. → I didn't see him yesterday
7. She goed to Tokyo last month. → She went to Tokyo last month
8. Did you went to the party? → Did you go to the party ?
9. They doesn't come to class yesterday. → They didn't come to class yesterday
10. We buy a new car last week. → We bought a new car last week.

### Part 3: Viết câu hỏi với Did (5 câu)

11. you / go / to school yesterday? → Did you go to school yesterday
12. she / finish / her homework? → Did she finish her homework?
13. they / watch / the movie last night? → Did they watch the movie last night?
14. he / call / you this morning? → Did he call you this morning.
15. Where / you / buy / that shirt? → Where did you buy that shirt?
