# Practice A8 - Future (will vs going to)

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn will hoặc going to (10 câu)

1. I think it __________ (rain) tomorrow. (no strong evidence)
2. Look at those clouds! It __________ (rain).
3. I’m tired. I __________ (go) to bed now.
4. We __________ (travel) to Da Lat next week. (plan booked)
5. This box is too heavy. I __________ (help) you.
6. He __________ (not/forget) your birthday. (promise)
7. Be careful! You __________ (drop) your phone!
8. A: The phone is ringing. B: I __________ (answer) it.
9. She __________ (study) abroad after graduation. (intention)
10. I’m sure you __________ (pass) the test. (general prediction)

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

11. I going to buy a new laptop next month. → __________
12. Look! The glass will fall! → __________
13. Will you going to join us? → __________
14. I won’t to be late. → __________
15. We will meet at 7 PM tomorrow. (plan fixed) → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **will rain**
2. **is going to rain**
3. **will go**
4. **are going to travel**
5. **will help**
6. **won’t forget**
7. **are going to drop**
8. **will answer**
9. **is going to study**
10. **will pass**

11. **I am going to buy a new laptop next month.**
12. **Look! The glass is going to fall!**
13. **Are you going to join us?**
14. **I won’t be late.**
15. **We are going to meet at 7 PM tomorrow.**

---

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 5 câu theo mẫu: **I will… (decision now)**
- Viết 5 câu theo mẫu: **I am going to… (plan)**
- Viết 5 câu theo mẫu: **Look! It is going to… (evidence)**
