# Practice C2 - This/That/These/Those + Articles

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn this/that/these/those (8 câu)

1. __________ book here is mine. (near)
2. __________ building over there is very tall. (far)
3. __________ shoes are dirty. (near, plural)
4. __________ cars in the parking lot are expensive. (far, plural)
5. Is __________ your phone? (near)
6. Are __________ your keys on the table? (near, plural)
7. I don’t like __________ noise outside. (far)
8. __________ pictures on my desk are from my trip. (near, plural)

### Part 2: Chọn a/an/the/Ø hoặc sửa cấu trúc (7 câu)

9. I bought ___ laptop yesterday. (first mention)
10. ___ laptop I bought is very fast. (specific)
11. This ___ laptop is very fast. (fix)
12. Those ___ students are from my class. (fix)
13. She wants ___ umbrella because it’s raining. (first mention)
14. ___ umbrella is in her bag now. (specific)
15. I like ___ music. (general, uncountable)

---

## Answer key

1. **This**
2. **That**
3. **These**
4. **Those**
5. **this**
6. **these**
7. **that**
8. **These**

9. **a**
10. **The**
11. **This laptop is very fast.**
12. **Those students are from my class.**
13. **an**
14. **The**
15. **Ø**

---

## Output (15 câu mô tả)

Viết **15 câu** mô tả đồ vật/tình huống quanh bạn. Checklist:
- Ít nhất 4 câu dùng **this/these** (near)
- Ít nhất 4 câu dùng **that/those** (far)
- Ít nhất 3 câu dùng **a/an**
- Ít nhất 3 câu dùng **the**
- Ít nhất 2 câu dùng **Ø** (plural/uncountable general)
