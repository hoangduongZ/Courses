# E1 – Passive (Level 1)
> Level: newbie • Focus: be + V3 • Use-case: IT/business/formal writing

---

## Purpose
Giúp bạn chuyển câu Active ↔ Passive để viết/đọc văn phong formal (report, email, docs) khi muốn **focus vào hành động/kết quả** hơn là người làm.

## Key points
- Công thức lõi: `be + V3 (past participle)`.
- Passive dùng khi: **(1) không biết/không cần nói ai làm**, **(2) muốn nhấn kết quả/quy trình**, **(3) văn phong trang trọng**.
- Tân ngữ của active → lên làm chủ ngữ của passive.
- Tác nhân (người làm) nếu cần: `by + agent` (*by the team*), nhưng thường bỏ.
- Chia thì nằm ở **be** (is/are/was/were/has been/will be…). V3 không đổi.
- Không phải động từ nào cũng chuyển passive: thường cần **transitive verb** (có object) như build, create, send, fix.
- Bẫy: dùng đúng V3 (sent, made, built, written…) và đúng singular/plural của `be`.
- Trạng từ (already/yet/just) trong passive vẫn đặt vị trí tự nhiên: thường giữa trợ động từ và V3 (*has been already sent* ít tự nhiên → *has already been sent*).

## Rule summary
### 1) Active → Passive (core)
- Active: `S + V + O`
- Passive: `O + be (same tense) + V3 (+ by S)`

### 2) Tense cheat sheet (Level 1)
- Present Simple: `am/is/are + V3`
- Past Simple: `was/were + V3`
- Present Perfect: `has/have been + V3`
- Future (will): `will be + V3`

## Examples
### A) Present Simple
1. Active: The team **updates** the website every week.
   Passive: The website **is updated** every week.

### B) Past Simple
2. Active: They **fixed** the bug yesterday.
   Passive: The bug **was fixed** yesterday.

### C) Present Perfect
3. Active: We **have sent** the email.
   Passive: The email **has been sent**.

### D) Future
4. Active: They **will deliver** the package tomorrow.
   Passive: The package **will be delivered** tomorrow.

### E) With agent (when needed)
5. Passive: The report was prepared **by** Anna.

### F) Common in IT/docs
6. The configuration file is stored in `/etc/app/`.
7. A new version will be released next week.

## Common mistakes
1. Sai: *The bug fixed yesterday.* → Đúng: **The bug was fixed yesterday.** (thiếu be)
2. Sai: *The email has sent.* → Đúng: **The email has been sent.** (perfect passive)
3. Sai: *The report was wrote by me.* → Đúng: **was written** (V3)
4. Sai: *The tasks is completed.* → Đúng: **The tasks are completed.** (plural)
5. Sai: *The meeting will be cancel.* → Đúng: **will be cancelled/canceled** (V3)
6. Sai: dùng passive với verb không có object: *He arrived → He was arrived* (sai). (arrive = intransitive)

## Practice A
> 15 câu (chuyển đổi/chọn/sửa lỗi).

### Part 1 — Active → Passive (6 câu)
1) They install the software every month.

2) The manager approved the request yesterday.

3) We have completed the task.

4) They will announce the results tomorrow.

5) Someone stole my bike last night.

6) The team is reviewing the document now. (gợi ý: `is/are being + V3`)

### Part 2 — Passive → Active (4 câu)
7) The email was sent this morning.

8) The meeting will be held on Friday.

9) The files have been uploaded.

10) The door is locked every night.

### Part 3 — Choose / Fix (5 câu)
11) Choose the correct sentence:
A. The report is write every week.
B. The report is written every week.

12) (Fix the sentence) *The problem has solved.*

13) Choose the best passive:
Active: “They clean the office every day.”
A. The office is cleaned every day.
B. The office was cleaned every day.

14) (Fix the sentence) *The tasks was completed on time.*

15) Choose the correct meaning:
“The package was delivered.”
A. Someone delivered the package (focus on doer).
B. The package arrived/delivery happened (focus on result).

## Answer key
1) The software **is installed** every month.
2) The request **was approved** yesterday.
3) The task **has been completed**.
4) The results **will be announced** tomorrow.
5) My bike **was stolen** last night. (agent unknown)
6) The document **is being reviewed** now.

7) Someone/The team **sent** the email this morning.
8) They **will hold** the meeting on Friday.
9) Someone/The team **has uploaded** the files.
10) Someone/The staff **locks** the door every night.

11) **B** — is written
12) **The problem has been solved.**
13) **A** — every day → present simple passive
14) **The tasks were completed on time.**
15) **B** — passive often focuses on result

## Practice B
> 5 bài viết (free writing).

1) Viết 6 câu passive ở Present Simple (quy trình/rule ở công ty hoặc lớp).
2) Viết 4 câu passive ở Past Simple (một sự cố đã được xử lý).
3) Viết 4 câu passive ở Present Perfect (việc đã hoàn tất).
4) Viết 4 câu passive ở Future (kế hoạch thông báo/phát hành).
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6–8 câu mô tả “release process” dùng ít nhất 6 câu passive (được phép dùng 1–2 câu active nếu cần).

## Self-check rubric
- Tôi dùng đúng công thức `be + V3` và chia thì ở `be`.
- Tôi phân biệt được khi nào nên bỏ `by + agent`.
- Tôi chọn đúng V3 (irregular verbs) và đúng singular/plural của `be`.
- Tôi không cố dùng passive cho động từ không có object (arrive, go, happen…).
- Tôi chuyển đổi Active ↔ Passive mà không đổi nghĩa chính.
