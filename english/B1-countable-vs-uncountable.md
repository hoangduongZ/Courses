# B1 – Countable vs Uncountable

## Purpose

B1 giúp bạn tránh lỗi rất hay gặp: **many/much**, **a/an**, **plural -s**, và các danh từ “nghe có vẻ đếm được nhưng thực tế không đếm trực tiếp” (information, advice, luggage…).

---

## Key points

- **Countable nouns (C)**: đếm được → **a/an**, **one/two**, **plural -s/-es**
  - a book / two books
- **Uncountable nouns (U)**: không đếm trực tiếp → **không a/an**, thường **singular verb**
  - information is… / advice is…
- **many** + C plural, **much** + U
- Khi muốn “đếm” uncountable → dùng **units/containers**:
  - a piece of advice, two pieces of information
  - a bottle of water, three cups of coffee
- **BẪY LỚN:** các uncountables “kinh điển”:
  - information, advice, furniture, equipment, luggage, homework, traffic, money, research, work

---

## Rule summary

### 1) Quantifiers
- **C plural:** many, (a) few, several, a number of
- **U:** much, (a) little, a bit of
- **Both:** some, any, a lot of / lots of, plenty of

### 2) Articles
- **a/an** chỉ dùng với **singular countable**
- **the** dùng cho cả C và U khi “specific/known”

### 3) Units for uncountables
- a piece of / two pieces of
- a bit of
- a bottle/cup/glass of
- a kilo/gram of

---

## Examples

### 1) Countable
1. I bought **a book**.
2. I bought **two books**.
3. There are **many reasons**.

### 2) Uncountable
4. I need **some information**.
5. I don’t have **much time**.
6. This **furniture is** expensive.

### 3) “Count” uncountables using units
7. Can you give me **a piece of advice**?
8. I have **two pieces of luggage**.
9. We need **three bottles of water**.

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| an information | some information / a piece of information | information = uncountable |
| many money | much money | money = uncountable |
| two advices | two pieces of advice | advice = uncountable |
| furnitures | furniture | furniture = uncountable |
| I have a homework. | I have homework. / a homework assignment | homework = uncountable |
| The informations are… | The information is… | information U + singular verb |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn many/much (6 câu)

1. I don’t have __________ time today.
2. There are __________ students in this class.
3. How __________ money do you need?
4. We don’t have __________ information about the problem.
5. She doesn’t drink __________ coffee.
6. I have __________ ideas.

### Part 2: Chọn a/an/some/Ø (5 câu)

7. I need ___ advice.
8. He bought ___ new phone yesterday.
9. We have ___ homework tonight.
10. She wants ___ apple.
11. Can you give me ___ information?

### Part 3: Viết lại dùng “a piece of / a bottle of …” (4 câu)

12. two advices → __________
13. three waters → __________
14. an information → __________
15. two luggages → __________

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết 10 câu: 5 câu dùng **many** + C plural, 5 câu dùng **much** + U.
2) Viết 10 câu dùng “unit”: a piece of / a bottle of / a cup of / a kilo of…
3) Viết 8 câu hỏi (How much…? / How many…?)
4) Viết 10 câu trộn: some/any/a lot of với cả C và U.
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6 câu mô tả đi siêu thị: cố tình dùng nhiều countable & uncountable.
