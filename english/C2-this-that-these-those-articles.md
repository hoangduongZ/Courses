# C2 – This/That/These/Those + Articles (combo cơ bản)

## Purpose

C2 giúp bạn chỉ đồ vật/tình huống **đúng khoảng cách + đúng số ít/số nhiều**, và tránh lỗi mạo từ khi đi kèm “this/that/these/those”. Đây là combo dùng cực nhiều trong giao tiếp và viết mô tả.

---

## Key points

- **This/That** = **singular** (số ít)
  - **this**: gần người nói
  - **that**: xa người nói
- **These/Those** = **plural** (số nhiều)
  - **these**: gần
  - **those**: xa
- “This/That/These/Those” có thể là:
  - **Determiner** (đứng trước noun): *this book, those cars*
  - **Pronoun** (đứng một mình): *This is nice. Those are expensive.*
- **Không dùng a/an/the ngay sau demonstrative determiner**:
  - ✅ *this book* / *that idea* / *these students*
  - ❌ *this a book* / *those the students*
- **Articles quick map**
  - **a/an**: 1 cái (lần đầu, không cụ thể) → *a book*
  - **the**: cụ thể/đã biết/duy nhất → *the book (we talked about)*
  - **Ø**: plural general / uncountable general → *Books are useful. Water is important.*

---

## Rule summary

| Distance | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| Near | **this** + singular N / this | **these** + plural N / these |
| Far | **that** + singular N / that | **those** + plural N / those |

### Articles with demonstratives
- **this/that/these/those + noun** (no a/an/the)
  - *this phone*, *those shoes*
- “a/an/the” dùng khi **không có demonstrative**
  - *a phone*, *the phone*

---

## Examples

### 1) Near vs far (singular/plural)
1. **This** chair is comfortable. (near, singular)
2. **That** chair looks old. (far, singular)
3. **These** cookies taste great. (near, plural)
4. **Those** cookies are too sweet. (far, plural)

### 2) Determiner vs pronoun
5. **This book** is interesting. (determiner)
6. **This** is interesting. (pronoun)
7. **Those students** are noisy. (determiner)
8. **Those** are noisy. (pronoun)

### 3) Articles vs demonstratives
9. I need **a** pen. (any pen)
10. I need **the** pen on the table. (specific)
11. I need **this** pen. (near + specific)

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| This books are mine. | These books are mine. | “books” plural → these/those |
| These book is good. | This book is good. / These books are good. | số ít vs số nhiều |
| This a phone is new. | This phone is new. | demonstrative + noun (không a/an/the) |
| Those are my a friends. | Those are my friends. | plural + không dùng a/an |
| That is an useful app. | That is a useful app. | “useful” /juː/ → a |
| I like the dogs. (nói chung) | I like dogs. | nói chung → plural Ø |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn this/that/these/those (8 câu)

1. __________ book here is mine. (near)
2. __________ building over there is very tall. (far)
3. __________ shoes are dirty. (near, plural)
4. __________ cars in the parking lot are expensive. (far, plural)
5. Is __________ your phone? (near)
6. Are __________ your keys on the table? (near, plural)
7. I don’t like __________ noise outside. (far)
8. __________ pictures on my desk are from my trip. (near, plural)

### Part 2: Chọn a/an/the/Ø hoặc sửa cấu trúc (7 câu)

9. I bought ___ laptop yesterday. (first mention)
10. ___ laptop I bought is very fast. (specific)
11. This ___ laptop is very fast. (fix)
12. Those ___ students are from my class. (fix)
13. She wants ___ umbrella because it’s raining. (first mention)
14. ___ umbrella is in her bag now. (specific)
15. I like ___ music. (general, uncountable)

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết 6 câu mô tả đồ vật quanh bạn: 2 câu **this**, 2 câu **these**, 1 câu **that**, 1 câu **those**.
2) Viết 6 câu dùng “this/that/these/those” như **pronoun**: *This is… / Those are…*
3) Viết 8 câu luyện mạo từ: 3 câu **a/an**, 3 câu **the**, 2 câu **Ø**.
4) Viết 6 cặp câu chuyển đổi: *a book → this book*, *the shoes → those shoes*.
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6 câu mô tả nơi bạn đang ngồi (near vs far).
