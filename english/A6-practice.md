# Practice A6 - Present Perfect (Experience / Result / Duration)

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền have/has + V3 (Experience) (5 câu)

1. I __________ (see) that movie three times.
2. She __________ never __________ (try) sushi.
3. __________ you ever __________ (be) to Thailand?
4. We __________ (meet) him before.
5. They __________ (not/read) this book.

### Part 2: just / already / yet (Result) (5 câu)

6. I have __________ (just/finish) my report.
7. He has __________ (already/send) the files.
8. We haven’t decided __________.
9. Has she called you __________?
10. They have __________ (not/arrive) __________.

### Part 3: since / for (Duration) (5 câu)

11. I have studied English __________ 2023.
12. She has worked here __________ two months.
13. We have known each other __________ a long time.
14. He has lived in this city __________ he was a child.
15. They have been married __________ 10 years.

---

## Answer key

1. **have seen**
2. **has / tried**
3. **Have / been**
4. **have met**
5. **haven’t read**

6. **just finished**
7. **already sent**
8. **yet**
9. **yet**
10. **haven’t arrived yet**

11. **since**
12. **for**
13. **for**
14. **since**
15. **for**

---

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 5 câu theo pattern: **Have you ever + V3…?**
- Viết 5 câu theo pattern: **I have just/already… / I haven’t … yet**
- Viết 5 câu theo pattern: **I have + V3 + since/for…**
