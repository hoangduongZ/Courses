# G4 – Error Correction (DEBUG GRAMMAR)

> Mục tiêu task: tự phát hiện & sửa lỗi theo nhóm: Tense / S-V / Articles / Pronoun / Preposition / Verb form.

---

## Purpose

Task này rèn kỹ năng “debug” bài viết/nói: nhìn câu là biết lỗi thuộc nhóm nào và sửa nhanh, đúng rule. Đây là cách tăng điểm rất mạnh vì bạn sửa được lỗi lặp lại (tense, S‑V, articles, prepositions…).

## Key points

- Luôn sửa theo checklist 6 nhóm: **Tense → S‑V → Articles → Pronoun → Preposition → Verb form**.
- Đọc câu và hỏi: “Có time word không?” → quyết định **tense** trước.
- Kiểm tra chủ ngữ số ít/số nhiều → **S‑V agreement**.
- Nhìn danh từ: đếm được/không đếm được; lần đầu/cụ thể → **articles**.
- Đại từ: chủ ngữ/tân ngữ/sở hữu → **pronoun**.
- Giới từ: học theo rule/collocation (at/on/in; depend on; interested in) → **preposition**.
- Verb form: sau modal = V1; sau preposition = V‑ing; gerund/infinitive theo động từ.
- Khi sửa: ưu tiên **sửa ít nhất** để câu đúng và tự nhiên.
- Ghi “rule” 1 dòng cho mỗi lỗi để nhớ lâu.

## Rule summary

Checklist 6 bước (đi từ trên xuống):
1) **Tense**: time markers? (yesterday/last/in 2020/since/for/now)
2) **S‑V**: he/she/it + V‑s; plural noun + V1
3) **Articles**: a/an (first/one of many), the (specific/known/unique), Ø (general)
4) **Pronoun**: I/me/my/mine; he/him/his...
5) **Preposition**: at/on/in; during/while; depend on...
6) **Verb form**: modal + V1; to + V1; prep + V‑ing

Format sửa (bắt buộc): **Sai ở đâu → Vì sao sai → Sửa đúng → 2 ví dụ mới**

## Examples

1) ❌ He go to school every day.
   - ✅ He **goes** to school every day.

2) ❌ I have lived here since two years.
   - ✅ I have lived here **for** two years.

3) ❌ She is interested on music.
   - ✅ She is interested **in** music.

4) ❌ I saw him since 2020.
   - ✅ I have known him since 2020.

5) ❌ Although it was late, but we kept talking.
   - ✅ Although it was late, we kept talking.

6) ❌ I enjoy to read.
   - ✅ I enjoy **reading**.

7) ❌ I bought the new phone yesterday. (ngữ pháp đúng, nhưng article có thể sai nếu “new phone” không cụ thể)
   - ✅ I bought **a** new phone yesterday. (lần đầu nhắc)

8) ❌ During I was studying, my friend called.
   - ✅ While I was studying, my friend called.

## Common mistakes

1) Trộn cause/result: ❌ Because..., so ... → ✅ Because..., ... / ..., so ...
2) Trộn concession: ❌ Although..., but ... → ✅ Although..., ...
3) Dùng sai `since/for`: `since + start point`, `for + duration`
4) Sai S‑V: he/she/it + V‑s; people/they + V1
5) Sai article: `a/an` cho lần đầu/1 trong nhiều; `the` cho cái cụ thể/đã biết
6) Sai cấu trúc: `during + noun` vs `while + clause`; `prep + V-ing`

## Practice A

Sửa mỗi câu theo format: **Sai ở đâu → Vì sao sai → Sửa đúng** (chưa cần 2 ví dụ mới). Mỗi câu có 1 lỗi chính.

1) He go to work at 8 a.m.
2) They is my classmates.
3) I am agree with you.
4) I have studied English since three years.
5) She didn’t went to class yesterday.
6) I enjoy to watch movies.
7) I have a information for you.
8) I saw him since 2020.
9) My father, who works in a bank is very strict.
10) This is the first time I meet her.
11) I will call you in Monday.
12) During I was cooking, my phone rang.
13) She is married with a doctor.
14) If I will have time, I will help you.
15) The book is on my bag.

## Answer key

1) **S‑V** → he + V‑s → ✅ He **goes** to work at 8 a.m.
2) **S‑V** → they + V1 → ✅ They **are** my classmates.
3) **Verb form/collocation** → agree (verb) không dùng “am” → ✅ I **agree** with you.
4) **Preposition (since/for)** → duration dùng `for` → ✅ I have studied English **for** three years.
5) **Verb form** → did + V1 → ✅ She didn’t **go** to class yesterday.
6) **Verb pattern** → enjoy + V‑ing → ✅ I enjoy **watching** movies.
7) **Articles + uncountable** → information không đếm được → ✅ I have **some** information for you. (hoặc: I have information for you.)
8) **Tense** → since + mốc đến hiện tại → Present Perfect → ✅ I have known him since 2020. (hoặc: I have seen him since 2020.)
9) **Punctuation (non‑defining)** → cần dấu phẩy đóng → ✅ My father, who works in a bank, is very strict.
10) **Tense** → “This is the first time” thường dùng Present Perfect → ✅ This is the first time I **have met** her.
11) **Preposition (time)** → on + day → ✅ I will call you **on** Monday.
12) **During/while** → while + clause → ✅ **While** I was cooking, my phone rang.
13) **Preposition** → married to → ✅ She is married **to** a doctor.
14) **Conditionals Type 1** → if‑clause không dùng will → ✅ If I **have** time, I will help you.
15) **Preposition (place)** → in + container → ✅ The book is **in** my bag.

3) Chữa đoạn sau (ít nhất 6 lỗi):
   - Yesterday I go to school at 7. I am interested on English so I study hard. Although I was tired, but I keep studying. I have lived here since two years.
