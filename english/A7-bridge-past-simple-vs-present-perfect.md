# A7 – Bridge 2: Past Simple vs Present Perfect (QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT)

## Purpose

A7 giúp bạn **không nhầm “đã từng/đã làm”**: khi nào dùng **Past Simple** (có mốc quá khứ rõ) và khi nào dùng **Present Perfect** (không mốc, nhấn liên quan tới hiện tại).

---

## Key points

- **Past Simple (V2/ed)** = việc xảy ra & kết thúc **ở quá khứ**, thường có **mốc thời gian**.
- **Present Perfect (have/has + V3)** = quá khứ **liên quan tới hiện tại** (experience/result/duration), thường **không nêu mốc quá khứ đã kết thúc**.
- **Rule vàng:**
  - Có **finished time** (yesterday/last/in 2020/2 days ago/when I was a kid) → **Past Simple**
  - Không có mốc, hoặc hỏi “đã từng chưa”, hoặc nhấn “tới giờ” → **Present Perfect**
- **Cặp câu hỏi kinh điển:**
  - **Have you ever…?** → Present Perfect (experience)
  - **When did you…?** → Past Simple (đòi mốc)
- **already/just/yet** thường đi với **Present Perfect**
- **BẪY #1:** dùng Present Perfect với “yesterday/last/in 2020” → sai.
- **BẪY #2:** câu hỏi bị trộn: *When have you…?* (thường sai) → phải là *When did you…?*
- **BẪY #3:** time expressions “chưa kết thúc” (today/this week/this year) có thể dùng **cả 2**, tùy ý nghĩa.

---

## Rule summary

| You mean… | Use | Form | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Có mốc quá khứ rõ/đã kết thúc | **Past Simple** | V2/ed; did + V1 | I **met** him **yesterday**. |
| Hỏi/đề cập trải nghiệm “đã từng” | **Present Perfect** | have/has + V3 | **Have** you ever **met** a celebrity? |
| Kết quả tới hiện tại | **Present Perfect** | have/has + V3 | I’ve **lost** my keys. |
| Nhắc chi tiết “khi nào/ở đâu” | **Past Simple** | V2/ed | I **lost** them **this morning**. |

**2-step decision:**
1) Có “finished time” hoặc câu hỏi cần mốc (when/what time/in which year) → Past Simple.
2) Nếu không, và ý là “tới giờ/đã từng/chưa” → Present Perfect.

---

## Examples

### 1) Experience → Present Perfect
1. I **have visited** Japan.
2. She **has never eaten** sushi.
3. **Have** you ever **used** Git before?

### 2) Past time marker → Past Simple
4. I **visited** Japan **in 2019**.
5. She **ate** sushi **last night**.

### 3) Present relevance: perfect first, then past details
6. I **have lost** my phone. (result now)
7. I **lost** it **on the bus**. (detail in past)

### 4) already/just/yet → Present Perfect
8. They **have already finished** the test.

### 5) “Today/this week” (unfinished time) → depends
9. I **have had** three meetings **today**. (tới giờ)
10. I **had** three meetings **today**. (nhìn lại ngày hôm nay như đã xong / ngữ cảnh kể lại)

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| I have seen him yesterday. | I saw him yesterday. | “yesterday” = finished time → Past Simple. |
| When have you started? | When did you start? | “When” đòi mốc → Past Simple. |
| I didn’t finish it yet. | I haven’t finished it yet. | “yet” (chưa) → thường dùng Present Perfect. |
| She has gone to Paris last year. | She went to Paris last year. | “last year” → Past Simple. |
| Have you ever went there? | Have you ever been/gone there? | Sau have/has dùng **V3**. |
| I’ve met him in 2020. | I met him in 2020. | “in 2020” là mốc quá khứ → Past Simple. |

## Practice A (Controlled – 30 câu, chọn đúng thì)

**Yêu cầu:** chọn **Past Simple** hoặc **Present Perfect** cho mỗi câu.

1. I __________ (finish) my homework already.
2. I __________ (finish) my homework last night.
3. __________ you ever __________ (try) Vietnamese coffee?
4. When __________ you __________ (try) Vietnamese coffee for the first time?
5. She __________ (not/see) this movie yet.
6. She __________ (not/see) this movie yesterday.
7. We __________ (live) here since 2022.
8. We __________ (live) there in 2022.
9. They __________ (just/arrive).
10. They __________ (arrive) 10 minutes ago.

11. I __________ (lose) my wallet. (I can’t find it now.)
12. I __________ (lose) my wallet on the bus this morning.
13. He __________ (be) to Singapore twice.
14. He __________ (go) to Singapore in 2018.
15. __________ you __________ (read) the email yet?
16. Did you __________ (read) the email this morning?
17. My team __________ (already/solve) the problem.
18. My team __________ (solve) the problem yesterday.
19. I __________ (not/meet) her before.
20. I __________ (meet) her at the party last week.

21. We __________ (have) three tests this week. (so far)
22. We __________ (have) three tests last week.
23. He __________ (work) at this company for 5 years.
24. He __________ (work) at that company for 5 years. (but he doesn’t work there now)
25. __________ you __________ (ever/forget) your password?
26. I __________ (forget) my password yesterday.
27. She __________ (already/send) the message.
28. She __________ (send) the message an hour ago.
29. I __________ (never/break) my phone.
30. I __________ (break) my phone when I was a student.

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết **10 câu** Present Perfect về trải nghiệm (ever/never/already/yet/just).
2) Viết **10 câu** Past Simple có time markers (yesterday/last/in 2020/ago).
3) Viết 6 cặp câu theo pattern: **Present Perfect (general) → Past Simple (details)**.
4) Viết 8 câu hỏi: 4 câu **Have you ever…?** + 4 câu **When did you…?**.
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6 câu kể 1 trải nghiệm: mở bằng Present Perfect, sau đó kể chi tiết bằng Past Simple.
