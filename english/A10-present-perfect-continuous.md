# A10 – Present Perfect Continuous (Optional)

## Purpose

Present Perfect Continuous giúp bạn nói về **một hành động bắt đầu trong quá khứ và kéo dài tới hiện tại**, nhấn mạnh **quá trình/độ liên tục** (đang làm và có dấu hiệu/ảnh hưởng tới hiện tại).

---

## Key points

- **Form:** have/has been + V-ing
- Dùng khi muốn nhấn mạnh:
  - **duration + continuity** (đã đang làm… được bao lâu)
  - **recent activity with present evidence** (vừa/đã làm… nên hiện tại có dấu hiệu)
- Hay đi với **since/for, recently, lately, all day, these days**
- **So sánh với Present Perfect (have/has + V3):**
  - Perfect = nhấn **kết quả/hoàn thành/số lần**
  - Perfect Continuous = nhấn **quá trình/liên tục**
- **BẪY #1:** dùng với **state verbs** (know, want, need, believe…) → thường không dùng continuous
- **BẪY #2:** quên “been”: *I have studying…* (sai)
- **BẪY #3:** nhầm “for/since”

---

## Rule summary

| Type | Form | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Positive** | have/has been + V-ing | I **have been studying** for 2 hours. |
| **Negative** | haven’t/hasn’t been + V-ing | She **hasn’t been sleeping** well. |
| **Question** | Have/Has + S + been + V-ing? | **Have** you **been working** today? |

---

## Examples

### 1) Duration (kéo dài tới hiện tại)
1. I **have been learning** English for two years.
2. She **has been working** here since 2021.
3. **Have** you **been waiting** long?

### 2) Recent activity with evidence (dấu hiệu hiện tại)
4. You look tired. **Have you been studying** all night?
5. The kitchen smells great. Someone **has been cooking**.

### 3) Compare with Present Perfect (V3)
6. I **have read** three chapters today. (result/amount)
7. I **have been reading** all afternoon. (process)
8. He **has fixed** the bug. (done)
9. He **has been fixing** the bug for hours. (process)

### 4) Typical time words
10. I **have been feeling** stressed lately. (feeling = OK in practice)

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| I have studying for 2 hours. | I have **been studying** for 2 hours. | Thiếu “been”. |
| She has been knowing him for years. | She has **known** him for years. | “know” là state verb → dùng Present Perfect. |
| I have been worked here since 2022. | I have been **working** here since 2022. | Sau been phải là V-ing. |
| He hasn’t been finish yet. | He hasn’t finished yet. | “finish” → thường nói kết quả (V3) + yet. |
| Have you been to Japan? (muốn hỏi trải nghiệm) | Have you **been** to Japan? | Experience dùng Present Perfect, không dùng continuous. |
| I have been living here since 3 years. | I have been living here **for** 3 years. | for + khoảng; since + mốc. |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền have/has been + V-ing (7 câu)

1. I __________ (study) for two hours.
2. She __________ (work) here since 2021.
3. They __________ (wait) for the bus for 20 minutes.
4. We __________ (practice) this topic lately.
5. __________ you __________ (sleep) well these days?
6. He __________ (not/feel) well recently.
7. My friends __________ (talk) about that movie all week.

### Part 2: Chọn Present Perfect hay Present Perfect Continuous (5 câu)

8. I __________ (read) three articles today. (result)
9. I __________ (read) all morning. (process)
10. She __________ (write) five emails so far. (result)
11. She __________ (write) emails since 9 AM. (process)
12. He __________ (live) in Hanoi for 10 years. (state-like)

### Part 3: Sửa lỗi (3 câu)

13. I have been know him for years. → __________
14. She has been study English since 2023. → __________
15. Have you been finish your homework yet? → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **have been studying**
2. **has been working**
3. **have been waiting**
4. **have been practicing**
5. **Have / been sleeping**
6. **hasn’t been feeling**
7. **have been talking**

8. **have read**
9. **have been reading**
10. **has written**
11. **has been writing**
12. **has lived** (state-like: live can be either, but “has lived” is safer as general fact)

13. **I have known him for years.**
14. **She has been studying English since 2023.**
15. **Have you finished your homework yet?**
