# Practice C0 - Subject–Verb Agreement

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Chọn đúng động từ (10 câu)

1. He __________ (work/works) every day.
2. They __________ (need/needs) more time.
3. My parents __________ (live/lives) in the countryside.
4. The teacher __________ (teach/teaches) English.
5. Students __________ (study/studies) hard before exams.

6. Information __________ (is/are) easy to share.
7. The news __________ (is/are) surprising.
8. There __________ (is/are) a lot of traffic today.
9. There __________ (is/are) many ways to solve this.
10. Either option __________ (work/works) for me.

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

11. She don’t like coffee. → __________
12. The team are winning. → __________
13. A number of books is missing. → __________
14. The number of employees are growing. → __________
15. Each students have a laptop. → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **works**
2. **need**
3. **live**
4. **teaches**
5. **study**

6. **is**
7. **is**
8. **is**
9. **are**
10. **works**

## Output (30 câu – đạt ≥85%)

**Điền dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc.**

1. My brother __________ (play) football every weekend.
2. His friends __________ (play) football with him.
3. The manager __________ (want) a quick update.
4. Our managers __________ (want) a quick update.
5. This app __________ (work) well on my phone.
6. These apps __________ (work) well on my phone.

7. Water __________ (be) important.
8. Money __________ (not/grow) on trees.
9. Furniture __________ (be) expensive.
10. The equipment __________ (need) maintenance.

11. The news __________ (be) on TV at 7.
12. Mathematics __________ (be) difficult for some students.
13. Physics __________ (not/be) easy.

14. There __________ (be) a new message for you.
15. There __________ (be) two new messages for you.
16. There __________ (be) a lot of reasons to learn English.
17. There __________ (be) plenty of time.

18. Each student __________ (have) an ID card.
19. Every lesson __________ (take) about 30 minutes.
20. Either of these plans __________ (sound) good.
21. Neither answer __________ (be) correct.

22. A number of people __________ (be) waiting outside.
23. The number of people __________ (be) increasing.

24. The police __________ (be) here. (police = plural)
25. People __________ (be) friendly in this town.

26. My family __________ (be) having dinner now. (treat as one unit)
27. The team __________ (need) a new strategy. (one unit)

28. The list of items __________ (be) on the table.
29. One of my friends __________ (live) near here.
30. Many of the students __________ (study) online.

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 10 câu: 5 câu he/she/it + V-s, 5 câu they + V1
- Viết 10 câu với uncountable nouns (information/advice/money/furniture)
- Viết 10 câu với each/every/either/neither
