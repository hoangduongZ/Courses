# C1 – Pronouns (I/me/my/mine; he/him/his…)

## Purpose

C1 giúp bạn dùng đại từ đúng vai trò trong câu: **chủ ngữ / tân ngữ / sở hữu / phản thân**. Fix được C1 sẽ hết các lỗi kiểu “Me go…”, “Give to I…”, “This is her book → This is hers…”.

---

## Key points

- 4 nhóm chính cần nhớ:
  1) **Subject pronouns** (chủ ngữ): I, you, he, she, it, we, they
  2) **Object pronouns** (tân ngữ): me, you, him, her, it, us, them
  3) **Possessive adjectives** (tính từ sở hữu + noun): my, your, his, her, its, our, their
  4) **Possessive pronouns** (đại từ sở hữu, đứng một mình): mine, yours, his, hers, its*, ours, theirs
- **Reflexive pronouns** (phản thân): myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves
- **Rule nhanh:**
  - Trước động từ (làm chủ thể) → **subject**
  - Sau động từ/giới từ (nhận tác động) → **object**
  - Trước danh từ → **possessive adjective**
  - Không có danh từ phía sau → **possessive pronoun**
- **BẪY #1:** “between you and I” (sai) → sau giới từ phải **object**: “between you and me”
- **BẪY #2:** nhầm **her** (adj) vs **hers** (pronoun)
- **BẪY #3:** nhầm “it’s” (it is) vs “its” (possessive)

---

## Rule summary

| Function | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Subject (chủ ngữ) | trước động từ | **She** works here. |
| Object (tân ngữ) | sau verb/prep | I called **him**. / for **us** |
| Possessive adjective | trước noun | This is **my** phone. |
| Possessive pronoun | đứng 1 mình | This phone is **mine**. |
| Reflexive | “tự mình” | I did it **myself**. |

**Mini map:**
- my + N / mine
- her + N / hers
- their + N / theirs

*Ghi chú:* “its” rất ít dùng kiểu “This is its.” (nghe lạ), thường dùng “its + noun”.

---

## Examples

### 1) Subject vs Object
1. **I** know Tom.
2. Tom knows **me**.
3. **They** invited **us**.

### 2) Possessive adjective vs Possessive pronoun
4. This is **my** laptop.
5. This laptop is **mine**.
6. Is that **her** bag? → Yes, it’s **hers**.

### 3) After prepositions
7. Please sit next to **him**.
8. This gift is for **them**.

### 4) Reflexive
9. She taught **herself** English.
10. We did it **ourselves**.

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|---|---|---|
| Me go to school every day. | I go to school every day. | Chủ ngữ dùng **I**. |
| Give it to I. | Give it to me. | Sau giới từ dùng **object**. |
| This is her. (ý là “của cô ấy”) | This is hers. | “hers” = đại từ sở hữu. |
| That is mine book. | That is my book. | “my” + noun. |
| It’s color is red. | Its color is red. | “its” = sở hữu; “it’s” = it is. |
| He did it by hisself. | He did it by himself. | Reflexive đúng là **himself**. |

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền đại từ đúng (10 câu)

1. __________ am a beginner, but __________ practice every day. (I / I)
2. Anna is my friend. I talk to __________ often. (she/her)
3. Tom and I are classmates. The teacher knows __________ well. (we/us)
4. __________ is raining today, so __________ stay at home. (It / we)
5. This is __________ phone. Please don’t take it. (my/mine)

6. This phone is not __________. It is __________. (my/mine)
7. Is this __________ jacket? – Yes, it’s __________. (your/yours)
8. They invited __________ to the party. (we/us)
9. I can’t find __________ keys. Did you see them? (my/mine)
10. The manager called __________ this morning. (I/me)

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

11. Between you and I, this plan is risky. → __________
12. This is mine bag. → __________
13. She gave the book to he. → __________
14. Its a good idea. → __________
15. They did the project by theirself. → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **I / I**
2. **her**
3. **us**
4. **It / we**
5. **my**

6. **mine / my**
7. **your / yours**
8. **us**
9. **my**
10. **me**

11. **Between you and me, this plan is risky.**
12. **This is my bag.** *(hoặc: This bag is mine.)*
13. **She gave the book to him.**
14. **It’s a good idea.**
15. **They did the project by themselves.**

## Practice B (Free writing – 5 đề)

1) Viết 10 câu giới thiệu bản thân và bạn bè: dùng **I/me/my/mine** + **he/him/his** + **she/her/hers**.
2) Viết 8 câu miêu tả đồ vật trong phòng: xen kẽ **my/your/his/her/their**.
3) Viết 6 câu theo mẫu: “This is my ___.” và “This ___ is mine.” (đổi chỗ để luyện my vs mine).
4) Viết 6 câu có giới từ: for/with/to/from/between… (luyện object pronouns).
5) Viết 5 câu dùng reflexive: myself/himself/ourselves…
