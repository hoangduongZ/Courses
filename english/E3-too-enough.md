# E3 – Too / Enough
> Level: newbie • Focus: nói “quá…” / “đủ…” đúng pattern • Tránh lỗi *too much + adj* / *enough + adj* sai vị trí

---

## Purpose
Giúp bạn diễn đạt chuẩn “quá … nên không thể …” và “đủ … để …” khi nói/viết (mô tả khả năng, điều kiện, yêu cầu), rất hay dùng trong đời sống và workplace.

## Key points
- `too` nhấn mạnh **quá mức** (thường gây kết quả xấu/không làm được).
- `enough` nhấn mạnh **đủ mức** (đạt điều kiện).
- Pattern quan trọng:
  - `too + adj/adv`
  - `adj/adv + enough`
  - `enough + noun`
- `too much / too many` đi với **noun**, không đi với adjective.
- `enough` đứng **trước noun** nhưng đứng **sau adj/adv**.
- `to + V` thường đi sau too/enough: `too … to V`, `… enough to V`.
- Bẫy: `too` ≠ `very`. `too` thường hàm ý “quá… (vấn đề)”.
- Bẫy: phân biệt `much/many` (noun) vs `adj` (tính từ).

## Rule summary
### 1) Too + adj/adv
- `too + adj/adv (+ to V)`
  - It’s **too cold to** go out.

### 2) Adj/adv + enough
- `adj/adv + enough (+ to V)`
  - She is **old enough to** drive.

### 3) Enough + noun
- `enough + noun (+ to V)`
  - We have **enough time to** finish.

### Bonus: too much / too many
- `too much + uncountable noun` (too much time)
- `too many + plural countable noun` (too many emails)

## Examples
### A) too + adj
1. This box is **too heavy**.
2. It’s **too noisy to** focus.

### B) adj + enough
3. The room is **big enough** for five people.
4. He isn’t **careful enough to** do this task.

### C) enough + noun
5. We have **enough money**.
6. Do you have **enough time to** talk?

### D) too much / too many
7. I spend **too much time** on social media.
8. He receives **too many emails** every day.

## Common mistakes
1. Sai: *He is too much tired.* → Đúng: **He is too tired.** (too + adj)
2. Sai: *This is too cold enough.* → Đúng: **This is too cold. / This is warm enough.** (không trộn 2 kiểu)
3. Sai: *She is enough smart.* → Đúng: **She is smart enough.** (enough đứng sau adj)
4. Sai: *We have time enough.* → Đúng: **We have enough time.** (enough đứng trước noun)
5. Sai: *too many money* → Đúng: **too much money** (money uncountable)
6. Sai: *I’m too busy to can help.* → Đúng: **I’m too busy to help.** (to + V1)

## Practice A
> 15 câu (điền/chọn/sửa lỗi). Chọn đáp án đúng nhất.

1) I’m ___ tired to go out. (too / enough)

2) This laptop is fast ___ for my work. (enough / too)

3) We don’t have ___ time to finish today. (enough / too)

4) The coffee is ___ hot to drink. (too / enough)

5) He isn’t old ___ to drive. (enough / too)

6) There are ___ people in this room. I can’t breathe well. (too many / too much)

7) I have ___ work this week. I’m stressed. (too many / too much)

8) She speaks clearly ___ for everyone to understand. (enough / too)

9) It’s ___ late to call him now. (too / enough)

10) Choose the correct sentence:
A. She is enough good to lead the team.
B. She is good enough to lead the team.

11) (Fix the sentence) *I have money enough to buy it.*

12) (Fix the sentence) *He is too much busy to help.*

13) Choose the correct meaning:
“This bag is too heavy to carry.”
A. I can carry it.
B. I can’t carry it.

14) Choose the best:
We have ___ chairs for everyone.
A. enough  B. too

15) Choose the best:
He’s ___ to understand this topic.
A. smart enough  B. enough smart

## Answer key
1) **too** — too tired to…
2) **enough** — adj + enough
3) **enough** — enough time
4) **too** — too hot to…
5) **enough** — old enough to…
6) **too many** — people (countable plural)
7) **too much** — work (uncountable)
8) **enough** — clearly enough
9) **too** — too late to…
10) **B** — good enough (adj + enough)
11) **I have enough money to buy it.** — enough + noun
12) **He is too busy to help.** — too + adj
13) **B** — too…to… = không thể
14) **A** — enough chairs
15) **A** — smart enough

## Practice B
> 5 bài viết (free writing) theo template.

1) Viết 5 câu dùng `too + adj + to V` (kết quả: không làm được).
2) Viết 5 câu dùng `adj + enough + to V` (đủ điều kiện làm được).
3) Viết 5 câu dùng `enough + noun + to V`.
4) Viết 4 câu: 2 câu `too much + uncountable`, 2 câu `too many + plural`.
5) Viết 1 đoạn 6–8 câu về “work/study schedule” bắt buộc có: `too`, `enough`, `too much/too many` (ít nhất 1 lần mỗi loại).

## Self-check rubric
- Tôi dùng đúng 3 pattern chính: `too + adj`, `adj + enough`, `enough + noun`.
- Tôi đặt `enough` đúng vị trí (sau adj/adv, trước noun).
- Tôi phân biệt đúng `too much` (uncountable) vs `too many` (plural countable).
- Tôi dùng đúng `to + V1` sau too/enough (không thêm can/must).
- Tôi không dùng `too` khi chỉ muốn nói “rất” (very).
