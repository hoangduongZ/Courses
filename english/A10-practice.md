# Practice A10 - Present Perfect Continuous

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền have/has been + V-ing (7 câu)

1. I __________ (study) for two hours.
2. She __________ (work) here since 2021.
3. They __________ (wait) for the bus for 20 minutes.
4. We __________ (practice) this topic lately.
5. __________ you __________ (sleep) well these days?
6. He __________ (not/feel) well recently.
7. My friends __________ (talk) about that movie all week.

### Part 2: Chọn Present Perfect hay Present Perfect Continuous (5 câu)

8. I __________ (read) three articles today. (result)
9. I __________ (read) all morning. (process)
10. She __________ (write) five emails so far. (result)
11. She __________ (write) emails since 9 AM. (process)
12. He __________ (live) in Hanoi for 10 years. (state-like)

### Part 3: Sửa lỗi (3 câu)

13. I have been know him for years. → __________
14. She has been study English since 2023. → __________
15. Have you been finish your homework yet? → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **have been studying**
2. **has been working**
3. **have been waiting**
4. **have been practicing**
5. **Have / been sleeping**
6. **hasn’t been feeling**
7. **have been talking**

8. **have read**
9. **have been reading**
10. **has written**
11. **has been writing**
12. **has lived**

13. **I have known him for years.**
14. **She has been studying English since 2023.**
15. **Have you finished your homework yet?**

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 5 câu theo mẫu: **I have been + V-ing + for/since…**
- Viết 5 cặp câu: **PP (result) vs PPC (process)**
