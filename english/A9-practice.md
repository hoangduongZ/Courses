# Practice A9 - Mix Tenses (Application)

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

**Điền đúng thì (Present Simple / Present Continuous / Past Simple / Past Continuous / Present Perfect).**

1. I usually __________ (study) English after dinner.
2. Right now, I __________ (review) A9.
3. My brother __________ (not/like) coffee, but he __________ (drink) it today.
4. Yesterday at 9 PM, we __________ (watch) a movie.
5. We __________ (watch) a movie when the lights __________ (go) out.

6. I __________ (never/try) Indian food.
7. She __________ (already/send) the email.
8. They __________ (not/finish) the test yet.
9. He __________ (live) here since 2021.
10. I __________ (meet) him last year.

11. While I __________ (cook), my friend __________ (call) me.
12. When I arrived, they __________ (talk) about the project.
13. This week, I __________ (have) three meetings so far.
14. I __________ (lose) my keys. (I can’t find them now.)
15. I __________ (lose) my keys yesterday, but I found them later.

---

## Answer key

1. **study**
2. **am reviewing**
3. **doesn’t like / is drinking**
4. **were watching**
5. **were watching / went**

6. **have never tried**
7. **has already sent**
8. **haven’t finished**
9. **has lived**
10. **met**

11. **was cooking / called**
12. **were talking**
13. **have had**
14. **have lost**
15. **lost**

---

## Output (3 paragraphs)

Viết **3 đoạn**, mỗi đoạn **5–7 câu**:

1) **Routine + now** (Present Simple + Present Continuous)
2) **Past story** (Past Simple + Past Continuous)
3) **Perfect** (Present Perfect: experience/result/duration)

### Template gợi ý

**Paragraph 1 – routine + now**
- I usually __________.
- I often __________.
- I __________ every day.
- Right now, I’m __________.
- At the moment, I’m __________.
- Today, I’m not __________.

**Paragraph 2 – past story**
- Yesterday/Last night, I was __________.
- It was __________, and I was __________.
- Suddenly, __________.
- I __________, and then I __________.
- While I was __________, __________.
- In the end, __________.

**Paragraph 3 – perfect**
- I have __________ for __________.
- I have __________ since __________.
- I have never __________.
- I have already __________.
- I haven’t __________ yet.
- So far, I have __________.

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 5 câu Present Simple (routine) + gạch time words
- Viết 5 câu Present Continuous (now) + gạch “now/at the moment”
- Viết 5 câu Past Continuous + when + Past Simple
- Viết 6 câu Present Perfect (2 experience + 2 result + 2 duration)
