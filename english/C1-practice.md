# Practice C1 - Pronouns

## Practice A (Controlled – 15 câu)

### Part 1: Điền đại từ đúng (10 câu)

1. __________ am a beginner, but __________ practice every day. (I / I)
2. Anna is my friend. I talk to __________ often. (she/her)
3. Tom and I are classmates. The teacher knows __________ well. (we/us)
4. __________ is raining today, so __________ stay at home. (It / we)
5. This is __________ phone. Please don’t take it. (my/mine)

6. This phone is not __________. It is __________. (my/mine)
7. Is this __________ jacket? – Yes, it’s __________. (your/yours)
8. They invited __________ to the party. (we/us)
9. I can’t find __________ keys. Did you see them? (my/mine)
10. The manager called __________ this morning. (I/me)

### Part 2: Sửa lỗi (5 câu)

11. Between you and I, this plan is risky. → __________
12. This is mine bag. → __________
13. She gave the book to he. → __________
14. Its a good idea. → __________
15. They did the project by theirself. → __________

---

## Answer key

1. **I / I**
2. **her**
3. **us**
4. **It / we**
5. **my**

6. **mine / my**
7. **your / yours**
8. **us**
9. **my**
10. **me**

11. **Between you and me, this plan is risky.**
12. **This is my bag.** *(hoặc: This bag is mine.)*
13. **She gave the book to him.**
14. **It’s a good idea.**
15. **They did the project by themselves.**

## Remedial Exercises (nếu cần)

- Viết 10 cặp: **my + noun / mine**, **her + noun / hers**, **their + noun / theirs**
- Viết 10 câu có giới từ (to/for/with/between) + object pronouns
- Viết 5 câu dùng reflexive (myself/himself/ourselves...)
