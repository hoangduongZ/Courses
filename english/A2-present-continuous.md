# A2 – Present Continuous

## Purpose

Present Continuous giúp bạn diễn tả **3 tình huống thực tế quan trọng**:
1. Hành động **đang diễn ra ngay lúc nói** (I'm typing now)
2. Tình trạng **tạm thời** trong giai đoạn hiện tại (I'm living with my parents these days)
3. Xu hướng **đang thay đổi** (More people are learning English online)

---

## Key points

• **Công thức:** am/is/are + V-ing (thêm -ing vào động từ)  
• **Thời điểm:** now, at the moment, right now, currently, these days, this week...  
• **BẪY LỚN:** State verbs (động từ chỉ trạng thái) **KHÔNG dùng continuous** → know, want, need, like, love, hate, understand, believe, have (sở hữu), see (nhìn thấy), hear (nghe thấy)...  
• **Spelling rules:** -ing thêm đuôi:
  - run → running (nhân đôi phụ âm cuối nếu 1 âm tiết + 1 nguyên âm + 1 phụ âm)
  - make → making (bỏ -e câm)
  - study → studying (giữ -y)  
• **Không dùng cho thói quen lặp lại** → dùng Present Simple  
• **Có thể dùng để phàn nàn** khi kết hợp với "always": He's always complaining!  
• **Nhận diện:** nếu câu có "every day/usually/often" → Simple, không phải Continuous

---

## Rule summary

| Subject | am/is/are | V-ing | Example |
|---------|-----------|-------|---------|
| I | am | working | I'm working now |
| He/She/It | is | studying | She's studying English |
| We/You/They | are | playing | They're playing football |

**Negative:** am/is/are + **not** + V-ing  
**Question:** Am/Is/Are + subject + V-ing?

---

## Examples

**Đang xảy ra ngay bây giờ:**
1. I'm reading a book right now.
2. She's talking on the phone at the moment.

**Tạm thời (giai đoạn hiện tại):**
3. I'm staying at a hotel this week.
4. He's working on a new project these days.

**Xu hướng đang thay đổi:**
5. More people are using smartphones.
6. The weather is getting warmer.

**Phàn nàn với "always":**
7. You're always forgetting your keys!

**Câu hỏi:**
8. Are you listening to me?

---

## Common mistakes

| ❌ SAI | ✅ ĐÚNG | Giải thích |
|--------|---------|------------|
| I'm knowing the answer | I know the answer | "know" là state verb, không dùng -ing |
| He working now | He's working now | Thiếu "is" |
| She is study English | She is studying English | Thiếu -ing |
| They are play football | They are playing football | Thiếu -ing |
| I'm go to school every day | I go to school every day | Thói quen → dùng Simple |
| Are you wanting coffee? | Do you want coffee? | "want" là state verb |

## Practice A (Controlled - 15 câu)

**Part 1: Điền am/is/are + V-ing (5 câu)**

1. I __________ (watch) TV right now.
2. They __________ (not/work) today.
3. She __________ (cook) dinner at the moment.
4. We __________ (stay) at a hotel this week.
5. __________ you __________ (listen) to me?

**Part 2: Chọn đúng/sai - sửa nếu sai (5 câu)**

6. I'm understanding this lesson. (Đúng/Sai?)
7. He's playing tennis now. (Đúng/Sai?)
8. They are wanting to leave. (Đúng/Sai?)
9. She work in the garden. (Đúng/Sai?)
10. Are you needing help? (Đúng/Sai?)

**Part 3: Chọn Present Simple hoặc Present Continuous (5 câu)**

11. I (drink / am drinking) coffee every morning.
12. Look! It (rains / is raining).
13. She (knows / is knowing) three languages.
14. We (have / are having) lunch right now.
15. He (always comes / is always coming) late! (phàn nàn)

---

## Answer key

1. **am watching** (I + am + V-ing, "right now" = đang xảy ra)
2. **aren't/are not working** (negative form, "today" = tạm thời)
3. **is cooking** (She + is, "at the moment" = đang xảy ra)
4. **are staying** (We + are, "this week" = tạm thời)
5. **Are ... listening** (question form)
6. **SAI** → I understand this lesson. ("understand" là state verb)
7. **ĐÚNG** ("play" là action verb, "now" = đang xảy ra)
8. **SAI** → They want to leave. ("want" là state verb)
9. **SAI** → She is working in the garden. (thiếu is + -ing)
10. **SAI** → Do you need help? ("need" là state verb)
11. **drink** (every morning = thói quen → Simple)
12. **is raining** (Look! = tín hiệu đang xảy ra → Continuous)
13. **knows** ("know" là state verb → Simple)
14. **are having** (right now = đang xảy ra, "have" = ăn → Continuous OK)
15. **is always coming** (phàn nàn → Continuous với "always")

## Notes

- **State verbs quan trọng cần nhớ:** know, want, need, like, love, hate, prefer, understand, believe, think (ý kiến), remember, forget, belong, own, have (sở hữu), see (nhìn thấy), hear (nghe thấy), seem, appear, feel (cảm thấy về ý kiến)
- **Exception:** một số state verbs CÓ THỂ dùng Continuous khi đổi nghĩa:
  - think (suy nghĩ) → I'm thinking about it
  - have (trải nghiệm/ăn) → We're having dinner
  - see (gặp) → I'm seeing the doctor tomorrow
- **Time markers Continuous:** now, right now, at the moment, currently, at present, these days, this week/month, today (trong context đang xảy ra)
