# G1 – Prepositions of Time (at / on / in, since / for, during / while)

> Mục tiêu task: dùng giới từ thời gian đúng và tránh các bẫy rất hay sai (đặc biệt `in/on/at` và `since/for`).

---

## Purpose

Task này giúp bạn dùng giới từ thời gian chính xác khi nói/viết về lịch trình, thời điểm, khoảng thời gian và hành động diễn ra trong một giai đoạn. Đây là lỗi “nhỏ nhưng lộ trình độ” trong writing/speaking.

## Key points

- `at` = **điểm thời gian** (giờ cụ thể, thời điểm): `at 7:00`, `at noon`, `at night`.
- `on` = **ngày/ngày cụ thể**: `on Monday`, `on 2 Feb`, `on my birthday`.
- `in` = **khoảng thời gian lớn**: `in 2026`, `in February`, `in the morning`, `in the 1990s`.
- `since` = **mốc bắt đầu** (start point) → thường đi với **Present Perfect**.
- `for` = **độ dài thời gian** (duration): `for two hours`.
- `during` + **noun** (during the meeting/summer) ; `while` + **clause** (while I was meeting...).
- Bẫy: `in the night` vs `at night` (thường dùng `at night` cho “ban đêm” nói chung).
- Bẫy: `on the weekend` (AmE) vs `at the weekend` (BrE) — task này chấp nhận cả hai, ưu tiên chọn 1 style nhất quán.

## Rule summary

**at / on / in**
- `at` + time points: `at 6 pm`, `at midnight`, `at the moment`.
- `on` + days/dates: `on Friday`, `on 2 February`, `on New Year’s Day`.
- `in` + months/years/parts of day: `in April`, `in 2025`, `in the morning`.

**since / for**
- `since` + start point: `since 2020`, `since last week`, `since I was a child`.
- `for` + duration: `for 3 days`, `for a long time`.

**during / while**
- `during` + noun: `during the lesson`, `during the holiday`.
- `while` + clause: `while I was studying`, `while we were eating`.

## Examples

1) I wake up **at** 6:30 every day.
2) We have a meeting **on** Monday.
3) She was born **in** 2001.
4) I usually study English **in** the evening.
5) I have lived here **since** 2020.
6) I have lived here **for** five years.
7) Please keep quiet **during** the exam.
8) **While** I was taking the exam, my phone rang.

## Common mistakes

1) ❌ I will see you **in** Monday.
   - ✅ I will see you **on** Monday.

2) ❌ I met him **at** 2 February.
   - ✅ I met him **on** 2 February.

3) ❌ I have studied English **since** two years.
   - ✅ I have studied English **for** two years.

4) ❌ I have studied English **for** 2020.
   - ✅ I have studied English **since** 2020.

5) ❌ During I was studying, my friend called.
   - ✅ **While** I was studying, my friend called.
   - ✅ **During** my study time, my friend called. (danh từ)

6) ❌ I sleep **in night**.
   - ✅ I sleep **at night**.

## Practice A

**A1 — Fill in the blanks (at / on / in)**

1) The class starts ___ 8:00.
2) I was born ___ 2000.
3) We usually meet ___ Friday.
4) She works best ___ the morning.
5) They got married ___ 14 June.

**A2 — Choose since / for**

6) I have known him ___ five years.
7) She has worked here ___ 2022.
8) We haven’t eaten anything ___ three hours.
9) I have been busy ___ this morning.
10) They have lived in Hanoi ___ a long time.

**A3 — Choose during / while**

11) Please don’t talk ___ the movie.
12) My phone rang ___ I was cooking.
13) I fell asleep ___ the meeting.

**A4 — Fix the sentence (error correction)**

14) I will call you in Monday.
15) During I was driving, it started to rain.

## Answer key

1) **at** — giờ cụ thể.
2) **in** — năm.
3) **on** — ngày trong tuần.
4) **in** — buổi trong ngày.
5) **on** — ngày/tháng cụ thể.

6) **for** — duration.
7) **since** — start point.
8) **for** — duration.
9) **since** — “this morning” là mốc bắt đầu (từ sáng tới giờ).
10) **for** — duration.

11) **during** — sau đó là noun (the movie).
12) **while** — sau đó là clause.
13) **during** — noun (the meeting).

14) ✅ I will call you **on** Monday.
15) ✅ **While** I was driving, it started to rain.
    - (hoặc) ✅ It started to rain **during** my drive.
